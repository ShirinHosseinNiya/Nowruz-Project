Ebi (Ebrahim Hamedi) is an Iranian singer and songwriter born in 1949 in Tehran, Iran.
He began his music career in the 1970s and became one of the most influential figures in Iranian pop music. After the 1979 revolution, Ebi continued his work in exile and has released numerous albums and performed in international concerts. His music combines traditional Persian elements with modern pop styles, and he is known for both romantic ballads and socially conscious songs.
